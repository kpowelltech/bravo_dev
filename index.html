WHAT

We provide a “set and forget” subscription window cleaning service. First service provides interior and exterior glass cleaning including screens, frames, tracks and sills. Half yearly and quarterly service is exterior only including screens, frames and sills. One time window cleaning: Exterior or interior and exterior includes frames and sills. Does not include tracks and screens. All comercial cleanups and hardwater removal are one time window cleaning price X2.
